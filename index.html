Short bio

I am a lecturer at the Department of Computer Sciences of the Faculty of Sciences of the University of Porto, as well as a researcher at INESC TEC.

My research interests lie in cryptography and distributed systems, with a specific focus on provable security, trusted hardware, and multiparty computation.
